# InsightPilot AI

InsightPilot AI is an intelligent sales assistant designed to analyze CRM data and help sales teams make faster and better decisions. The system processes CRM opportunity data to identify risks, summarize performance, and recommend next best actions for sales representatives.

This project is inspired by the concept of AI-powered enterprise assistants such as Salesforce AgentForce.

---

## Problem Statement

Sales representatives spend excessive time manually searching through CRM records and emails to understand account history and opportunity data. This results in delayed responses, missed opportunities, and inefficient sales workflows.

There is a need for an AI-powered assistant that can automatically analyze CRM data, provide insights, and help sales teams take the right actions quickly.

---

## Proposed Solution

InsightPilot AI analyzes CRM datasets and transforms them into clear business insights for sales teams.

The system helps users understand opportunity data, detect risky deals, and generate recommendations for improving sales performance. It also provides an AI assistant interface that allows users to ask questions about their CRM data.

---

## Key Features

- Executive KPI Dashboard showing total deals, high-risk deals, top region, and top industry
- Revenue analytics by region
- Deal stage distribution visualization
- Automatic detection of high-risk deals
- AI-generated sales insights and recommendations
- AI assistant for querying CRM data
- Interactive dashboard for sales analytics

---

## Approach

The system follows a simple AI-assisted analytics workflow:

1. The user uploads a CRM dataset.
2. The system processes the dataset and extracts key information such as deal value, probability, and activity history.
3. Data analytics modules calculate performance metrics and detect risky opportunities.
4. The system generates insights and recommendations based on the analyzed data.
5. The AI assistant allows users to interact with the system using natural language queries.

This approach enables sales teams to quickly understand CRM data and make informed decisions.
